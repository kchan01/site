Hello. My name is Kevin Chan. I am currently a third year at the University of California, Berkeley (Go Bears) and I am studying Molecular and Cell Biology. This site is dedicated to all of my travels and the photos I have taken over the past 5 years. Over the past 15 years, I have shot on various cameras (ranging from point and shoot to DSLRs), taken the equivalent of 1 terabyte of photos and videos, and visited 21 countries. I currently shoot with a Canon 5D MkII and a Canon EOS M. My favorite photography subjects range from portraits, to long exposures, to nature. Some other hobbies that I enjoy include swimming (only during warm weather), playing video games, or drawing. On campus I am heavily involved in the service community and am currently the 2019 President of Cal Berkeley Habitat for Humanity. If you'd like to get in touch, please head over to the contact tab top left. Otherwise checkout my travels and my photos!
